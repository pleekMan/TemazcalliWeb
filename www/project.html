The Project

Bio, Credits, Research and Making - Of

Agustín Ramos Anzorena

Electronic Artist and creative coder that enjoys exploring the fields of New Media, artistic abstraction, visual composition and electronic play.
With a Master in Technology and Aesthetics of Electronics Arts, a background in 3D Animation and graphic design, he is interested in creating installations and software with an emphasis in the visuals, constantly discovering new areas and subjects to explore, still fascinated by the expressive power of code and the poetry hidden in algorithmic thinking. In parallel, he relishes in the composition of soundScapes and sound experimentation.

Mario Guzman

Experimental writer and Media Artist interested in merging languaje and visual phenomena in the realms of web development, electronic art and robotics.
With an Arts Degre in [ Digital ] Literature and a Master in Technology and Aesthetics of Electronic Arts, his work explores different means of communication between human and non-human, but also between ways of encoding perception and narrative through technology. He specialises in the areas of experimental cinema, spirit photography, non-linearity, mapping and interactive media.

Culture and Art

Temazcalli (from the nahuatl temazcalli: ‘house where you sweat’. temaz, ‘sweat’ and calli, ‘House’) is a steam bath used in traditional medicine and everyday life by the people of central Mexico.



Software: The Huichol Pattern Generator

The Huichol Pattern Generator is a custom software created to aid in the generation of the colorfull patterns present in the art of the Huichol, a Mexican MesoAmerican culture.
Their art usually depicts animals and symbols from their mythology, constructed with very colorfull geometric patterns, highly saturated palettes, and composed with beads arranged over an hexagonal grid that completely covers the artwork.
